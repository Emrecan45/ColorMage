ColorMage Emrecan45

ColorMage is a platform-puzzle game: you play a Mage who can change color to travel through levels full of platforms, traps and enemies. The golden rule: you can only interact with elements that match YOUR color, the others become intangible and you pass right through them.

🎨 Play with color

Grab potions to change your color, walk on blocks of your hue, phase through the rest, and reach the yellow portal to clear the level.

🔥 Features

  • 10 handcrafted levels across two worlds, with more on the way.
  • A cast of enemies and traps that each demand their own approach.
  • Powers and secrets to discover along the way.
  • A Grimoire that fills up as you uncover the world.
  • A shop to unlock new avatars and upgrade your abilities.
  • Best times saved for every level: beat your record!

🕹️ Controls

  • Arrow keys to move, Up to jump, E to shoot (keys are rebindable).
  • Playable with keyboard on PC and touch controls on mobile/tablet.

Good luck, Mage. The Prism has shattered... it's up to you to reclaim your colors.
